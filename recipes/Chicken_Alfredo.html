Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken alfredo is my all time favorite

A creamy white sauce, long strands of pasta

mixed with tender pieces of lean protein

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces dried fettuccine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (about 1 pound total)
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, divided
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 8 tablespoons (1 stick) European-style unsalted butter, divided
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup finely grated Parmesan cheese (2 1/2 ounces)
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • Coarsely chopped fresh parsley leaves, for serving.

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta, drain, and reserve cooking water
  2. Dry the chicken and season with salt and pepper
  3. Heat the oil over medium-high in a large frying pan
  4. Add the chicken to the hot pan, and cook for 5 to 7 minutes
  5. Flip the chicken and cook for another 5 to 7 minutes, until the chicken reaches 165°F
  6. Slice the chicken and cover to keep warm
  7. Melt the rest of the butter and sauté the garlic
  8. Make the Alfredo sauce
  9. Add the drained pasta and toss to coat in the sauce
